FOCUS: Object-Centric World Models for Robotics Manipulation

Anonymous Authors

Abstract

Understanding the world in terms of objects and the possible interactions with them is an important cognition ability, especially in robotics manipulation. However, learning such a structured world model, which also allows to accurately control the agent, remains a challenge. In this work, we propose FOCUS, an object-centric world model, which allows for solving robotic manipulation tasks more efficiently. FOCUS also enables consistent exploration of object interactions, by exploiting our novel object-centric exploration strategy.
